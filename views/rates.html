    • Frequently Asked Questions

    Here are some answers to questions that have come up often over the time we been doing this:

    1) Do you provide insurance?

    We cannot provide insurance for you. We ask that you check with your personal insurance provider to see if you are already covered for a street legal 2016 Polaris rzr 1000, to ride offroad. If not, see if you can acquire coverage during the time of your rental. Unfortunately we cannot rent our RZR to you in you are unable to do so due to liability reasons.

    2) Do you require a security deposit?

    We do require a security deposit of $500 with proof of insurance, and $1000 without. The deposit will be fully refunded upon the safe return of the rented vehicle, minus any damages.

    3) How many people can go on your RZR?

    This 2016 Polaris RZR XP 1000 seats and buckles 4 persons of any age. Bring a car seat to buckle in with smaller children under the age of 3 (we may be able to accommodate you if needed). We also recently acquired a "bump seat" to place in between the front 2 seats for toddlers if wanted, also bringing to total seats to 5.

    4) Do you provide helmets or other riding gear?

    We don’t typically offer this. Most riding won’t require helmets, but we do recommend goggles or glasses to keep the dirt and wind out of your eyes. If you need a few helmets, we may be able to accommodate you with our own. The full front windshield keeps much of the wind and dirt out of your eyes as well.

    5) Who can drive your RZR?

    Only the person(s) on the rental agreement can drive the RZR. It is illegal for anyone under 16 to operate the RZR on any city or county roads. We do not allow anyone under the age of 25 to rent or drive our RZR.

    6) Does the RZR have any storage space for things like coolors, etc?

    Yes, the RZR has a "trunk" over the engine where you can tie down coolers, blankets, etc.

    7) Do you offer multi-day discounts?

    Yes, that is something we are willing to do. Please ask us when you call what our multi-day rates are.

    3) Where can we ride the RZR?

    There are tons of places to ride RZRs in Utah, and we are continually discovering more. Check out our social media channels for videos and posts of where we have been! Also, check out SidebySideLife.com as a great resource on trails, UTVs, and more!
